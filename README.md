I'm a full-stack engineer who builds products end-to-end — architecture, backend, frontend, deployment. Not just features, the whole thing.

Right now I'm building **BikeTribe**, a real-time community platform for motorcycle riders with live ride tracking, gang systems, and social layers. Before that I shipped **FreelanceFlow**, a SaaS dashboard for freelancers managing clients and income.

I work best when I own meaningful parts of a system — not just tickets. If you're building something and need someone who can think about it at a product level while writing the actual code, I'm your person.
